Author

Victor Black

Victor Black is an author of dark fantasy, progression fantasy and speculative fiction. Crafting worlds that blur the line between the sublime and the sinister, his stories pull readers into realms of wonder and shadow. Each tale is a descent into the extraordinary — where heroes and monsters are often one and the same, and the truth is always darker than it seems.

Ongoing Novella

Sky Pillar

It cannot walk. It cannot speak — not at first. It has no sword, no master, no sect willing to take it as a disciple. What it has is time, stillness, and roots that drink deep from the spiritual veins of the world itself. While human cultivators chase after pills and techniques, scrambling and scheming and dying young from greed, the tree simply grows — patiently, inexorably, in every direction at once.
